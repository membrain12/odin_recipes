Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1(3.3 ounce) package instant white chocolate pudding mix
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda, salt, and instant pudding powder; set aside
  3. In medium bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Blend in the eggs and vanilla.
  4. Gradually mix in dry ingredients until just blended and stir in chocolate chips using a wooden spoon
  5. Scoop cookies using an ice cream scoop. Place cookies at least 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Bake 8-10 minutes until lightly golden. Let cool on baking sheets

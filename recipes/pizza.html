Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe gets back to the basics and simply makes a good pizza with simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 ounces grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 500Â°F
  2. Roll out dough on lightly floured surface
  3. Transfer dough to large cast-iron and add toppings
  4. Cook for 6 minutes then rotate to cook evenly until crust is golden brown
  5. Slice and serve!

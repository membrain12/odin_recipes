Creamy Chicken Alfredo

Description

This creamy alfredo recipe removes the time of making your own sauce from scratch and instead opts to spice up store bought alfredo sauce instead

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds bonesless chicken breast cut into chunks
  • 2(16 ounce)packages fettuccine pasta
  • 4(15 ounce)jars Alfredo sauce
  • 2 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Season chicekn with salt and pepper and cook until done on medium/high heat. Roughly 7-10 minutes
  2. Cook fettuccine in boiling water. Roughly 8-10 minutes
  3. Add alfredo sauce to chicken along with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, basil, salt, black pepper, onion powder, and cayenne.
  4. Bring to slight boil and stir in Parmesean cheese. Cook mixture until cheese is melted. Roughly 3-5 minutes
  5. Drain cooked pasta. Scoop into serving bowls and pour sauce on top
